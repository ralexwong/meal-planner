How to use the planner

  • Tap on the breakfast/lunch/dinner buttons to checkout some pre assorted meals! Click and drag one of the meals into a box.

  • You can also search for specific food with our search button!

  • The planner will calculate the nutritional info of your daily meal when box has a food item in it.
